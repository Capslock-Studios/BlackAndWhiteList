    We store only publically available information

    To register a person:

    1. You don't need to have an account but you will have a WB Number assocated with your ID and Phone number.

    2. Listing a person is not completely free. We charge R147 for a BLACK LIST and WHITE LISTING is free.

    3. You are required to upload proof of the claims in your Compaints. Proof can be a document, voice note, video, screen shot etc.

    4. You can list a person that you are happy about. e.g. Getting good service from a waiter.

    5.if you are black listed you can remove your BLACK LISTING by paying what you owe (if anything is owed)to the person that listed you plus a R1000 admin fee.
